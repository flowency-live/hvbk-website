About us

Hi, I'm Sarah. Your books, in safe hands.

Welcome to The Hi Vis Bookkeeper, specialist bookkeeping for construction, trades and contractor businesses across the UK. I'm Sarah, the founder and lead bookkeeper, helping business owners stay on top of bookkeeping, VAT returns, CIS compliance, management reporting, clean-up projects and Making Tax Digital.

Based in Stockport, Cheshire, I work with clients remotely throughout the UK, providing accurate, reliable support that helps businesses save time, stay compliant and make informed decisions. Alongside me are Charlie and Lola, the friendly faces who keep me motivated and remind me why strong client relationships matter.
