About us

Welcome to The Hi Vis Bookkeeper

Specialist bookkeeping services for construction, trades, and contractor businesses across the UK. I'm Sarah, the founder and lead bookkeeper, helping business owners stay on top of their finances. Whether it's monthly bookkeeping, VAT returns, CIS compliance reviews, MTD or clean-up projects, we are here to help.

Based in Stockport, but working with clients remotely across the UK, providing accurate, reliable bookkeeping support that helps businesses save time, maintain compliance, and make informed financial decisions.

Alongside me are Charlie and Lola, the friendly faces behind the business who keep me motivated and remind me why building strong relationships with clients matters.

At The Hi Vis Bookkeeper, we believe bookkeeping should be straightforward, proactive, and tailored to the needs of busy businesses. Whether you're a small business owner, sole trader, subcontractor, contractor, or growing construction company, our goal is to help you stay organised, compliant, and focused on running your business while we take care of the numbers.

How we work

Straightforward, Proactive and On Your Side

At Hi Vis Bookkeeping, we believe bookkeeping should be simple, accurate and tailored to the needs of individual businesses. Whether you're a sole trader, subcontractor, contractor or growing limited company, you'll receive proactive support designed to keep your finances organised, compliant and up to date.

Beyond day-to-day bookkeeping, we provide clear financial analysis and management reporting to help you understand your cash flow, monitor business performance and make informed decisions with confidence. Our goal is to give you greater visibility over your finances while reducing the administrative burden, so you can focus on delivering projects, growing your business and staying profitable.
